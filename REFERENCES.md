# References & Credits

## Manufacturing — OEE

- **[Lean Production — OEE](https://www.leanproduction.com/oee/)** — OEE, its three
  factors, and the Six Big Losses.
- **[ISO 22400-2:2014](https://www.iso.org/standard/54497.html)** — the international
  standard defining manufacturing KPIs, including availability, performance, quality,
  scrap ratio and rework ratio.
- **Seiichi Nakajima, *Introduction to TPM* (Productivity Press, 1988)** — the origin
  of OEE.
